Welcome. You've found something a little different.

This is a project about the forces reshaping our world — exponential technologies, the changing role of human labor, and the economic assumptions we've built everything on that may no longer hold. The kind of ideas usually locked behind academic jargon or lost in political noise, brought here in plain language for anyone willing to think about what comes next.

You'll also find games, experiments, and stories about how all of this gets built — because this entire site is a collaboration between a human (an engineer, not a software engineer) and Claude, Anthropic's AI assistant. Every page, every line of code, every design choice emerged from that conversation. The process is part of the point.

This isn't a finished product. It's a living project — part essay collection, part workshop, part invitation. Explore what's here, question what you find, and if something sparks an idea, there may be a place for you in what this becomes.
